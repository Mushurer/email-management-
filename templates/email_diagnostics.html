Troubleshooting Tips
Common Issues for Gmail:
  • Use App Password: For Gmail accounts, generate an app-specific password instead of your regular password
  • Enable 2FA: Two-factor authentication must be enabled to create app passwords
  • Check SMTP Settings:
    • Server: smtp.gmail.com
    • Port: 587
    • Encryption: STARTTLS
  • Account Status: Ensure the Gmail account is active and not suspended
  • Less Secure Apps: App passwords are more secure than enabling less secure app access
To Generate Gmail App Password:
  1. Go to Google Account Security settings
  2. Enable Two-step verification (2FA)
  3. Go to App passwords section
  4. Select "Mail" from the dropdown or create custom name
  5. Copy the 16-character app password
  6. Use this password in the email settings (not your regular Gmail password)
